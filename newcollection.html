Gibson Les Paul Standard '60s Bourbon Burst

Singlecut Electric guitar with curly maple top, Burstbucker 61 pickups and Grover tuners, case included

With the ' Gibson 60s Les Paul Standard Tribute Bourbon Burst , the Original Collection presents Gibson USA an electric guitar that authentically captures the spirit and sound of the innovative Singlecut models from the early 1960s. Accordingly, the Gibson '60s Les Paul Standard has a massive mahogany body without weight relief and a curly maple top in superior AA quality. With the historically correct " Slim Taper " profile, the glued mahogany neck also lies comfortably in the hand and offers optimum playing conditions across the entire rosewood fingerboard. Equipped with -V Alnico magnets, the Gibson Burstbucker 61 pickups on the guitar amp deliver an authentic PAF-style sound of distinctive midrange and endless sustain, while the hand-wired circuitry with Orange drop capacitors supports harmonic treble roll-offs. Last but not least, the Gibson '60s Les Paul Standard has precise Grover Rotomatic tuners, a ABR -1 Tune -O-Matic bridge and a tailpiece made of Aluminium .
